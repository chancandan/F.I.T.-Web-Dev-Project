Gift Ideas and Packs

Looking for the perfect gift idea for that special someone?

New
Charcuterie Gift Pack

When we set out to build a charcuterie pack, we wanted the ultimate in flavor variety. We started with the savory: Classic summer sausage from our own Barron Sausage Co. and Smoked Gouda. We then added the rich and sharp: 8-year-aged cheddar, the Italian-style Fratello, and our limited edition Door County Duet. Finally, we topped it off with the sweet: cherry lavender jam from Wisconsin's own Bushel and Peck. A perfect assortment for wine and cheese parties or a gourmet gift.

New
Tuscan Fontina Cheese

Shopping for something distinct? Planning to buy Fontina cheese? Look no further than this limited-edition cheese, hand-rubbed in an irresistible blend of Tuscan spices. Every meal will feel like a special occasion when you add this Italian Fontina cheese to your charcuterie board or fresh pasta dish.

New
Jalapenno Pepper Cheese Spread & Dip

This spicy (but not ﻿too spicy!) cheese spread is the perfect addictive snack to put out for gatherings or enjoy as a flavorful snack on your own. Made using real cheese and jalapeños in our facility in Kerry, this artisan creation is the real deal. Enjoy with pretzels, crackers, or spread on corn on the cob (trust us, it's really good!).
